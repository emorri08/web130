About Elly

I have been studying Web Design & Development here for two years. This is my 9th quarter but I spent the first two doing pre-reqs so I still have a little bit left to do here. I recently applied to the App Development BAS program at North Seattle College, and I am hoping to start that in the fall while I finish the requirements for my Web Design AAS-T at Seattle Central.

I live in Seattle with my husband and our three very spoiled tabbies. I love animals (especially cats) and was that kid who brought home all the neighborhood stray and injured animals. I am a fairly vocal advocate for the need for more and better TNR programs as well as low cost spay/neuter programs, and the push to ban declawing.

In what little spare time I have, I like to read & spend time with the cats. I still have a close relationship with both of my parents and I try to spend time with them as often as our schedules allow. I do try to talk to them both a few times a week when we aren't able to visit in person. I also enjoy photography, but I have not been able to get out to take photos as much as I would like to in the past few months.

I also enjoy watching movies (Office Space and Spinal Tap are a couple personal favorites), sketching, embroidery, crochet (even though I am not very good at it), and making candles.
